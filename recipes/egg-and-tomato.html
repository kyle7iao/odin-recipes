Egg and Tomato Recipe

Description

Egg and tomato is a classic that everyone loves. It involves scrambled eggs mixed with tomatoes and is often eaten with rice.

I have a lot of good memories of eating this because my dad likes to make it. It is super yummy and healthy!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasoning (like scallions, salt, pepper, soy sauce, whatever you have on hand)

Steps

  1. Scramble those eggs!
  2. Add tomatoes in and cook till it looks okay
  3. Season it so it doesn't taste bland
  4. Put it over rice and eat it all
