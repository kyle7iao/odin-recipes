Rice Recipe

Description

Rice is the best source of carbohydrates to ever exist. I eat rice with almost everything.

I will never get tired of rice. It is simple to make, delicious, and pairs well with everything.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Any seasoning you want

Steps

  1. Wash rice
  2. Put rice in rice cooker with water
  3. Let the rice cooker work its rice cooking magic
